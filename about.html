Andi Draws Things

About

I'm Andi, and I am a digital artist. I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. Ever since I picked up a pencil, I have worked towards learning new techniques, and improving my own art style. In recent years, I realized that I want a career in the field of something I love: art and video games. One of my goals is to become a character concept artist for a video game company. Growing up, I spent my weekends and summers playing video games, which quickly became a main source of inspirations for my drawings.
